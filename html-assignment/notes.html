HTML Tags I Have Learned

Headings
Heading tags are numbered, ordered by most to least important. This is done by sizing the heading by their number (1,2,3...)
Emphasis
Emphasis is used to show important text. The reader will usually experience a change in tone when reading text that is emphasized.
Superscript Text
Superscript text allows text to be superscripted, which means it will appear attached but over top of text. This tag is very useful when writing a date: February 7th
Line Break
Line breaks are used mainly in poems and addresses.
Image
The image tag displays an image on a webpage. However, it needs two additional attributes for it to work. These attributes are "src" and "alt".
