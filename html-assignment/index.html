About Me

Hello, I'm Morgan, I am 20 years old (born January 24th, 2002). My family is from Newfoundland. However, I was raised in Ottawa. I went to SHHS and graduated in 2020. I took a year off to work at Pet Valu. Working there helped me gain confidence and bring my true personality out.

My greatest passion is animals. In the past year, I've become very fond of reptiles. I own three geckos, all different species. Though my favourite animal will always be dogs. I've loved them since I was born. I've researched them for my entire life and have learned to understand them. I hope to someday have a second job working with dogs.
